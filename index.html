  • Grug Brained Dev
  • Stevey's Google Platform Rant
  • You and Your Research
  • Salary Negotiation: Make More Money, Be More Valued. (By patio11)
  • Levels is the best salary comparison website
  • The Tao of Programming
  • Geeks, MOPs, and sociopaths in subculture evolution
  • Google I/O 2012 - The Art of Organizational Manipulation
  • Paul Graham: The Submarine (it's about PR firms)
  • Forgotten Employee
  • Competative programmers handbook
